# Audit Readiness

This section demonstrates internal audit preparation and self-assessment activities aligned with ISO/IEC 27001:2022 requirements.  
The objective is to evaluate ISMS implementation, identify gaps, and define corrective actions for continual improvement.

## Purpose
- Assess conformity of the ISMS against ISO/IEC 27001:2022
- Verify implementation of policies and controls
- Identify nonconformities and improvement opportunities
- Track corrective and preventive actions

## Scope of Audit
The internal audit (self-assessment) covers:
- ISMS scope and context
- Risk assessment and risk treatment
- Statement of Applicability (SoA)
- Information security policies
- Incident response and business continuity
- Vendor risk management controls

## Methodology
The audit was performed using:
- Document review of ISMS artefacts
- Checklist-based control verification
- Mapping against ISO clauses and Annex A controls
- Recording findings and corrective actions in structured logs

## Artefacts in This Section

### 1. Internal Audit Plan
Defines audit scope, audit areas, schedule, and evidence to be reviewed.

### 2. Internal Audit Checklist & Results
Checklist mapped to ISO/IEC 27001 clauses and Annex A controls with conformity status (C, NC, OFI).

### 3. Audit Findings Log
Documents identified nonconformities and opportunities for improvement with severity and ownership.

### 4. Corrective Action Plan (CAPA)
Tracks corrective and preventive actions including root cause analysis, target dates, and verification status.

### 5. Internal Audit Summary Report
Provides a consolidated summary of audit results, key findings, and improvement actions.

## Audit Type
This audit represents a simulated internal audit / self-assessment conducted as part of a portfolio project.  
No external certification audit was performed.

## Outcome
- ISMS conformity was partially achieved
- Key gaps were identified and recorded
- Corrective actions were defined to improve ISMS maturity
- Inputs are provided for management review and continual improvement

## Disclaimer
All audit artefacts are based on a simulated organization and are created for learning and portfolio demonstration purposes only.  
No real organizational data is included.
