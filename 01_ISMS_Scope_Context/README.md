# ISMS Scope and Context
## Organization Overview
A simulated Digital Payments and AML Technology organization providing transaction monitoring and regulatory reporting services to financial institutions.
## ISMS Scope
The ISMS includes:
- AML transaction monitoring systems
- Payment processing platforms
- Customer and transaction databases
- Cloud infrastructure (AWS)
- Corporate IT systems
- Third-party vendors supporting operations
- ## Exclusions
- - Physical ATM infrastructure
- Non-IT marketing tools
- ## Interested Parties- Regulators (RBI, GDPR authorities)
- Customers (banks and NBFCs)
- Employees
- Technology vendors
- External auditors
## Information Security Objectives
- Protect sensitive AML and financial data
- Ensure availability of critical systems
- Maintain regulatory and contractual compliance
